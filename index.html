Welcome to the official route to becoming a Marketing Major at the University of Georgia! I am currently a first year student in Athens, and wanted to construct a cite to not only keep me on track over the years, but also to benefit future intended marketing majors who may not know where to start course wise. I am someone who loves to be organized so having my credits already achieved from high school, current courses, and a link to the CRNs of courses I need to take to graduate will be an efficient resource to go back and forth as I register for classes each semester. I. Terry College of Business

"As the first business school in the South, the Terry College of Business is steeped in a tradition of providing cutting-edge business education to its students. Our challenging curriculum, exceptional faculty, and close-knit alumni network ensure that Terry students are prepared to become the next generation of leaders."

Source: http://www.terry.uga.edu/about

Terry is a very competitive school within the University of Georgia so it is imperative to figure out the requirements needed to apply, and to apply as soon as one can. A typical time for a Intended Marketing major to apply to the business school is in the Fall of their second year. This being, many students will take classes online over the summer to get the credits needed to be illegible.
